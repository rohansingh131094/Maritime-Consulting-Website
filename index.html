About

Four decades at the helm of India's most demanding maritime programs.

Girideep Singh is a former Commodore of the Indian Navy and former Chairman & Managing Director of Hindustan Shipyard Limited. Across his career he led naval engineering, ship repair, submarine refit, strategic projects, finance and infrastructure — carrying programs from approvals and funding through yard execution, trials and delivery.

Having completed that institutional chapter, he now works independently with defence, maritime and infrastructure leaders who want a senior operator in the room: someone who has been personally accountable for schedule, budget, safety and stakeholder confidence on programs where the margin for error is small.

He advises selectively, takes a hands-on view of execution, and is candid about what a program needs to succeed.

Experience

From naval engineering command to enterprise-level shipyard leadership.

As Chairman & Managing Director and Director of Strategic Projects at Hindustan Shipyard Limited, Girideep Singh led across ship repair, submarine division, strategic naval projects, finance and infrastructure during a critical institutional transition.

Earlier, as Commodore and senior engineering leader in the Indian Navy, he oversaw aircraft carrier projects, repair yard operations, submarine building programs, fleet maintenance and naval dockyard production teams.
